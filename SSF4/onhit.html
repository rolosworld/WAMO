What Are My Options?

On Block · On Hit

This little program tells you the possible links that can be used after connected attacks. Possible links are calculated using the frame data. It may not work because of distance, character position, throw... etc.

Attacker: Got Hit:
  • Green: Best
  • Yellow: Medium
